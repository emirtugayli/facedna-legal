Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions (iOS) or Google Play > Payments & subscriptions (Android) to cancel or modify your plan.

Is my photo data stored?

No. Photos are sent to the AI for analysis in real time and are never stored on any server. Results are saved locally on your device only.

Why does the app need camera access?

FaceDNA uses your camera to capture a photo for facial analysis. You can also select an existing photo from your library. Camera access can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

How many analyses can I do per day?

Free users can perform up to 2 analyses per day by watching a short rewarded ad before each analysis. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited analyses with no ads.

I purchased Premium but it's not showing. What should I do?

Go to Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. This will sync your subscription status. If the issue persists, contact us at facedna.app@gmail.com.

How accurate is the analysis?

FaceDNA uses AI to provide entertainment-based facial analysis. Results are generated by artificial intelligence and should be treated as fun, not scientific fact.
