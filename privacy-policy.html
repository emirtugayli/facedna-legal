Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

FaceDNA ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application that analyzes facial features using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

FaceDNA requests access to your device camera or photo library to capture or select a facial photo for analysis. The photo is sent to the Google Gemini API for AI-based processing.

  • Facial photos: Used solely for analysis. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on any server.
  • Device identifiers: Anonymous identifiers may be collected by third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) for analytics and ad personalization.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered facial analysis via the Google Gemini API.
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To manage subscriptions and in-app purchases through RevenueCat.

3. Data Storage & Security

Photos submitted for analysis are not stored on our servers. They are transmitted securely to the Google Gemini API, processed, and immediately discarded. Analysis results are stored locally on your device only.

4. Third-Party Services

FaceDNA integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API — AI facial analysis processing. Terms
  • Google AdMob — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

5. Camera Permission

The App requests camera access to allow you to take a photo for analysis. You may also select an existing photo from your library. Camera access can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

6. Advertising

Free users may see rewarded advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

7. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat and processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

8. Children's Privacy

FaceDNA is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
