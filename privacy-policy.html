Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

FaceDNA ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application that analyzes facial features using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Face Data Collection and Usage

Due to the nature of our application, we handle your facial images ("Face Data"). We want to be completely transparent about how this sensitive data is managed:

  • What data we collect: When you use the FaceDNA analysis feature, the app collects the facial image you provide either via your device camera or photo library. We do not collect 3D depth data, biometric maps, or continuous facial tracking data.
  • How we use it: This image is used strictly and exclusively to generate your "DNA/Ethnicity" resemblance analysis results.
  • Disclosure and Third-Party Sharing: To provide this AI-powered analysis, we securely transmit your photo to our third-party AI processing partner (Google Gemini API) in real time.
  • Data Retention: Your photo is retained only for the few seconds required to perform the analysis. It is permanently deleted immediately afterward. We do not store, log, or cache your face data on any of our servers, nor does our third-party partner use it for training their models.

2. Other Information We Collect

  • Device identifiers: Anonymous identifiers may be collected by third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) for analytics, subscription verification, and ad personalization.

3. How We Use Other Information

  • To provide AI-powered facial analysis via the Google Gemini API.
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To manage subscriptions and in-app purchases through RevenueCat.

4. Data Storage & Security

As explicitly stated above, Face Data (photos submitted for analysis) are never stored on our servers. They are transmitted securely to the Google Gemini API, processed momentarily, and immediately discarded. Your analysis results are stored locally on your device only.

5. Third-Party Services

FaceDNA integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API — AI facial analysis processing. Terms
  • Google AdMob — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

6. Camera Permission

The App requests camera access to allow you to take a photo for analysis. You may also select an existing photo from your library. Camera access can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

7. Advertising

Free users may see advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

8. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat and processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

9. Children's Privacy

FaceDNA is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
